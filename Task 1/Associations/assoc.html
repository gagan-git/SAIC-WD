IBGAA | IIT BHU GLOBAL ALUMNI ASSOCIATION

IIT BHU Global Alumni Associaton (IBGAA) is the Association of Alumni of IIT-BHU, Varanasi. It includes all alumni, including BENCO, MINMET, ITBHU and other incarnations of the engineering college of BHU. Our alumni association is involved in various activities, which require funding and generous support from our alumni. The major activities of our association are Alumni-fumded scholarship for needy students; Maintaining coordial realtions with students, alumni, faculty and administration, Supervise meetings at chapter level and global meet.

AIBA | ASSOCIATION OF IIT BHU ALUMNI

Major activities and main objectives of Association of IIT BHU Alumni (AIBA) are to promote socio-culture interaction between alumni and their family, to build an active network of alumni around the world, to help the institute to collaborate with the industry for better placements and faculty upgrade, to create an awareness about the achievements, contribution and the rich heritage of the institute within the media as well as the industry.
