Centenary initiatives launch

22 Feb 2018

Summary

Share :

The Indian Institute of Technology BHU had a grand beginning of the centenary year on Thursday in the Swatantrata Bhawan. On this occasion, about 60 alumni from different departments of 1968 batch celebrated the legacy Golden Jubilee.

During this time, the 'Logo', 'Backdrop' of the centenary year and the Vision of the Institute ''Higher Education Produced with Character '' was unveiled. Organizing Committee President, Prof. RK Mishra welcomed the guests and said that there are only some prestigious educational institutions in the country that have completed 100 years in which the name of the IIT (BHU) has also been added. The institute will organize a grand ceremony at Basant Panchami in 2019 to celebrate its centenary year.

The Institute's DoRA Prof. AK Tripathi said that in the year 1919, the Banaras Engineering College was established, in 1968, the Institute of Technology, Kashi Hindu University. Then in 2012 the status of Indian Institute of Technology was found. He said that the main focus of the institute is to lined up and develop all the departments so that they can cooperate with the industries and reach the next level. He said that as a part of the centenary celebrations for the good of the Institute, the institute has a great opportunity to launch new projects. On this occasion, Prof. Rajeev Sangal, Director of the Institute welcomed all the former students and guests who attended the function. At the same time, members of the Alumni Interaction Cell, managed to make the event a success. The institute's alumni like Arvind Gupta, CEO of Dual Hydeppact, and Rahul Sharma of Amazon Web Series, including teachers, employees and a large number of students were also present. In the evening, cultural events were organized in the Swatantrata Bhavan. In it, dance, song-music and drama stage, poetry celebrations were also organized by the Clubs of Cultural Council.

Alumni appreciated the efforts of Director and Deans of Institute to maintain its academic excellence, its plans to get into newer areas of research and for greater scientific outreach through society-related programmes. The Student Alumni Interaction Cell had put in diligent efforts to successfully plan and execute the launch event along with the Golden Jubilee Alumni Meet. This event was covered by media and broadcasted live.
